Classic Avocado Toast

Our Classic Avocado Toast is a customer favorite. Enjoy freshly toasted sourdough bread topped with creamy avocado, cherry tomatoes, a sprinkle of sea salt, and a dash of lemon juice. It's a perfect start to your day, offering a healthy and delicious balance of flavors.

Pair it with our freshly brewed coffee or a refreshing smoothie for a complete breakfast experience at Brewberry Cafe.

Classic Cappuccino

Our Classic Cappuccino is a rich and aromatic coffee drink that combines the boldness of freshly brewed espresso with the creaminess of steamed milk, topped with a light layer of frothy milk foam. Perfectly balanced, each sip delivers a harmonious blend of robust coffee flavor and velvety texture.

Whether you’re looking to kickstart your day or enjoy a relaxing afternoon break, our Cappuccino is a timeless choice that pairs well with a fresh pastry or a moment of quiet reflection at Brewberry Cafe. Indulge in this café classic, made just the way you like it—available in both regular and decaf options.

Fresh Blueberry Muffin

Start your day with our Fresh Blueberry Muffin, a delightful treat bursting with juicy blueberries and a hint of vanilla. Each muffin is baked to golden perfection, with a tender, moist crumb that melts in your mouth. The sweet and tangy blueberries are complemented by a lightly crisp, sugar-dusted top, making every bite a perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Ideal for breakfast on the go or as a midday snack, our Blueberry Muffin pairs beautifully with a hot cup of coffee or a refreshing glass of iced tea
